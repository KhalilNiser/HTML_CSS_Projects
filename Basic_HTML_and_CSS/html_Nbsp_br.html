Music. Spirit & Body Health

Anirud Jaidev Anand (aka Russill Paul)

 Russill Paul is a world-renowned musician
as well as a teacher of Eastern spirituality

When the Creator fashioned the human body, the
human soul refused to enter because it didn’t
want to take on the body’s limitations. The
Creator then began to play music. In order to
feel the fullness of this music through the
senses and receptivity of the human form, the
soul was coaxed into the body. — Russill Paul
relaying an ancient Sufi story in The Yoga of Sound.

Joe Elliott

  Joseph Thomas Elliott (born 1 August 1959) is
an English singer-songwriter, best known as
the lead singer and one of the founder members
of the hard rock band Def Leppard

At age eight, "Joe Elliott Lead Singer
for the Heavy Metal Band Def Leppard" wrote
and recorded his first song, entitled "Going
Away Forever". Today, he still has the recording
on his mom's tape recorder. At age eleven, he
made his stage debut by miming Elvis Presley
playing guitar at a talent show. Soon after that
he learned to play six-string himself.
